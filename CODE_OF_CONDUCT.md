# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a harassment-free contributor experience regardless of
background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

Be respectful, discuss technical tradeoffs in good faith, give actionable
feedback, and avoid personal attacks or unwanted attention. Maintainers may
remove content or participation that threatens safety or productive
collaboration.

Report conduct concerns privately using the security contact documented in
`SECURITY.md`. Maintainers will review reports promptly and protect reporter
privacy where practical.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
